FAQs


    1) When can meeting rooms be booked?

    Monday – Friday, 7am – 9pm and Saturday, 9am – 5pm.
    For Sunday options please contact us directly.

    Our Conference rooms can be booked by the full-day or half-day only and are exceptionally popular, so availability is a lot more limited.


    3) Do you have laptop and projectors available for hire?

    We have a limited number of laptops and projectors to hire - please include these when you make your booking.


    4) Are there DVD playing facilities in the room?

    Unfortunately we do not currently have DVD playing facilities to provide but, you are welcome to bring your own equipment, should you wish.


    5) Do the rooms have Wi-Fi?

    All meeting rooms have password protected Wi-Fi. This is free to use with your meeting room booking – please ask if you would like to access the Wi-Fi and we can set you up with the password.


    6) Is parking available at this venue?

    Parking is not available at EZ-Rooms.


    7) Does EZ-Rooms provide catering?

    EZ-Rooms does not provide any catering.
